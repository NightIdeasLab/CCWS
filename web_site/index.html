Speaky For Robots Experiment

Welcome to the Speaky for Robots (S4R) web site for experimental evaluation!

What is Speaky for Robots?

Speaky for Robots is a research project funded by the UE within the ECHORD FP7 context, and it is carried out by two partners: Sapienza University of Rome, and Mediavoice S.r.l.

Our Goal

Speaky for Robots (S4R) aims at fostering the definition and deployment of voice user interfaces (VUIs) in robotic applications where human-robot interaction is required. S4R promotes speech technologies transfer towards manufacturing processes, to provide semi-automatic speech-based interface development for robotic platforms.

For additional information, please refer to the project web site.

Why am I here?

You are here because you can:

  • contribute to the final demonstrator of the S4R project
  • support us in acquiring an extensive lexical corpus to control mobile wheeled robots
  • your contribution will be adopted in RoboCup@Home competitions
How long does it take?

It will take you 10 minutes.

What should i do?

The experiment consist of two parts. First, the system will randomly select for you four high level tasks that the robot should accomplish. For each of them, you will be free to decide, pronounce and record the best sentence to command the robot. Second, the system will present to you four sentences already recorded by someone else which you will have to repeat.

Please join the experiment! Thanks for your patience.
